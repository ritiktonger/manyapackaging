Old Coguratted Boxes

Old Corrugated Boxes (OCC) refer to used corrugated cardboard containers that have served their original purpose and are now ready for recycling or reuse. These boxes are typically made from sturdy, multi-layered kraft paper, featuring a fluted or ridged inner layer that provides strength and durability. Commonly used for packaging and shipping, OCC materials are a valuable source of recyclable fiber in the paper industry.
